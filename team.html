Prashant Patil

Partner

Prashant is a veteran banker who has extensive experience in working with private banks. He has over 24 years+ experience in funding for large corporates under various structures. His expertise lies in funding under various debt structure.

Prashant has helped many companies for growth and structured funding as well. He has raised more than INR 1000 crores of debt in various structures like project finance, working capital, term debts and supply chain funding. He has raised funds from various PSUs, private banks and NBFC.

Kanchan Bhide

Partner

Kanchan Bhide is a Chartered Accountant by qualification. Having worked with Tier-1 CA firms for 3 years at the start of her career, she got an opportunity to work on a large size of fund-raising round for a listed company in 2011. From past 12 years Kanchan handheld SMEs and Mid Corporates from varied Industries in raising finance from Nationalized, Private and Leading Cooperative Banks along with NBFCs.

Kanchan has helped companies in various sectors from Forging to Hospitality and Distillery to ITES to raise debt. Coming from a business background, she understands the complexities of any business and the importance of optimum level of funding for businesses to achieve their goals.
